WHAT WE DO

Validators

We partner with leading blockchain protocols and companies to host blockchain validators as a part of giving back to the blockchain community.

Workshops

We host weekly workshops centered around Ethereum and Decentralized applications, focusing on the basics of solidity and smart contracts.

Accredited Courses

We design open source accredited blockchain courses taught at UC Davis, introducing blockchain technology to undergraduates as a part of their coursework.
